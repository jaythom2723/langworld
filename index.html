Langworld

A worldbuilding project

About

Langworld is a simple yet convoluted and complicated worldbuilding project that spans thousands of "years". The world itself is constantly evolving as new ideas are curated and refined over time.

Despite this, the end-game scenario of all of the cultural, linguistic, and geographical "evolution" are all in the name of providing a realistic world with an actual history behind it. Historical events and what-not will have to be thought of either during or after a given era has ended. However, historical events will primarily be significant in nature as opposed to insignificant.
